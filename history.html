Our History

Explore the rich history of the Pictou Academy Education Foundation.

Year 1: Foundation Established

In this year, the Pictou Academy Education Foundation was established to support the educational endeavors of Pictou Academy students.

Year 2: First Bursary Awarded

The first bursary was awarded to a deserving student, marking the beginning of our mission to support student success.

Year 3: Expansion of Programs

We expanded our programs to include funding for school trips, sports teams, and other extracurricular activities.
