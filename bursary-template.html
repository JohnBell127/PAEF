Scammell Bursary

Supporting students in their academic pursuits at Pictou Academy.

Purpose

The Scammell Bursary aims to provide financial support to students who demonstrate academic excellence and community involvement.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a current student at Pictou Academy.
  • Must demonstrate financial need.
  • Must have a minimum GPA of 3.0.
  • Must be involved in extracurricular activities or community service.

Application Process

To apply for the Scammell Bursary, please complete the following steps:

  1. Fill out the bursary application form.
  2. Submit a personal statement (maximum 500 words) explaining your academic achievements, extracurricular involvement, and financial need.
  3. Provide two reference letters, with at least one being from an academic reference (e.g., a teacher or guidance counselor).
  4. Submit your application and supporting documents to the school guidance counselor by the specified deadline.

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: March 31, 2024
  • Notification of Award: May 15, 2024

Contact Information

If you have any questions about the Scammell Bursary, please contact the Pictou Academy Education Foundation at info@paef.org or call (555) 123-4567.
