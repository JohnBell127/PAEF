About Us

Learn more about the Pictou Academy Education Foundation and our mission.

Our Mission

The mission of the Pictou Academy Education Foundation is to provide financial assistance to Pictou Academy students through bursaries, scholarships, and other funding opportunities. We strive to support the academic and personal growth of students by ensuring they have access to necessary resources and opportunities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to foster a community where every student at Pictou Academy has the opportunity to achieve their full potential. We aim to empower students by alleviating financial barriers and promoting educational excellence.
